Setup Error

You are seeing this bit of content because you did not correctly setup your base.html template. Luckily, the fix is simple.

How To Fix

The controller {{ controller.get_class_name }} defined in {{ controller.get_class_path }} is attempting to use {{ controller.chrome_template }} for its layout chrome. If you do not have your own {{ controller.chrome_template }} defined in your template directory, add it. Depending on your setup, this will probably be in home/templates/base.html or templates/base.html

Here is a sample base.html for you to use:
